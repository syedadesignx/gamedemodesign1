Choose your DARK race:
Drakiir are the rarest of the Dark Races and, aside from adventurers, are seldom found anywhere except the deepest caverns or fallen archaeopolises. This Race is instinctively knowledgeable about the world, which is said to be because they are descended from Dragons themselves in some way. Unfortunately, the entire Race are also dying from a Magical plague of unknown origin. Many adventurers hope to find a cure to this plague. Drakiir have a Racial +1 Intelligence Modifier.
