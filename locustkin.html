Locustkin are a plentiful Light Race equally at home in settlements and in the wildlands. They tend to be enthusiastic about whatever their current task, looking for the optimistic stance on any situation. This can lead to underestimating or overlooking problems. Locustkin have a Racial +1 Willpower Modifier. Locustkin require rare, specially-made armour due to their unusual shape.
